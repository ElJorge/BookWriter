### Beat 4 — The Over-Talker Meets the Plain-Speaker (the human weld, register-clash)
**Structural summary:** Theo approaches Rhea and OVER-talks — jokes, a refill, the patter that makes strangers feel held. **Character annotations:**
- **THEO**
  - Subtext: patter is armor; he reaches for it because the alternative is the silence he's terrified of. He's trying to give warmth to someone asking for proof — and he has no proof, only himself, and he doesn't yet know that's the point.

  - Voice cadence: HOT patter — refill offer, a joke about the stool, three sentences where one would do. Then, crucially, it SLIDES OFF and he can HEAR it sliding (the post-keystone awareness without yet the post-keystone discipline).
  - Physical expression: bartender hands — a glass, a pour, a coaster — all the moves that work on everyone, doing nothing here.
- **RHEA**
  - Subtext: she registers his kindness and sets it aside; kindness has been offered before and the hands still dropped her. She is not cold — she's CORRECT, and that's worse for Theo.
  - Unspoken want: to be given a reason to believe that isn't a feeling. Stated want: proof the chain won't drop her.
  - Voice cadence: PLAIN, COMPLETE, economical. She says the whole true thing and stops. Her objection is a verification objection: "many hands means many hands that can let go." Name CLEAN.
  - Physical expression: she doesn't touch the refill. Stillness as argument.

**Relationship dynamic:** THE HEART-CLASH. Over-sayer vs. plain-complete-sayer — opposite relationships to disclosure, both alone. Charm does NOT work (lock this). Power sits with Rhea: she's immune to his only tool.
**Dialogue direction:** Comedy = the toolkit failing in real time (every move that's worked on a thousand strangers, useless). Ache = he wants to give warmth, she wants proof. **Dialogue direction:** ONE warm beat total — THIS. Wound-as-credential, NOT confession-for-comfort. Rhea's turn rendered through Theo's reading. KEEP IT PARTIAL — threshold, not resolution. Thesis lands through Theo's perception: becoming a chain IS the weapon. Hope with a blade in it.
