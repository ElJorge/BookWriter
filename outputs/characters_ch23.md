**Character annotations:**
- **Donna (POV)** — THE LONE WARM BEAT. Protect it.
  - Subtext: This is love rendered as a command. The warmth is in the SENDING, not in stopping them — stopping them would dishonor the vote. She is the anti-collector: he would not feel this at all. The crack is grief swallowed into competence.

  - Physical expression: Render the dock-callback obliquely — her hands do the OPPOSITE work now (once she pulled a man IN; now she opens her hand to send two OUT). A hand that reaches toward Cole/Russ and converts into a point toward the door. The grief lives in the converted gesture.
  - The anti-collector contrast: lives in her READ of her own hands vs. his — she sends with friction (it costs her); he approves without friction (it costs him nothing). Do NOT let her name the contrast aloud.

**Relationship dynamic:** Donna ⇄ Cole & Russ — the thesis made flesh: consent honored, hands read true, sent WELL. The crew witnesses; no one comments (commenting would break it).
**Dialogue direction:** The chapter's ONE warm beat — keep it SMALL and re-sealed into a cue. No speech. The crack must convert into the order. Dock-callback oblique (hands doing opposite work). Contrast with collector unspoken.
